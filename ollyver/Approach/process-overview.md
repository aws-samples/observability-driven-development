# Ollyver Observability Agent Workflow Overview

## The Workflow:
• **Agent Initialization** → **Gap Detection** → **Automated Remediation** → **Continuous Monitoring**

## Your Role:
• **Launch development environment** (VSCode + Q Dev CLI)
• **Interact with Ollyver** using natural language commands
• **Review and approve** automated observability enhancements
• **Add custom requirements** when needed for specific business needs
• **Important**: Ollyver works alongside you - you maintain control while gaining automated assistance

## Organizational Integration:
• **Standards Enforcement**: Ollyver applies your organization's observability requirements automatically
• **KPI Integration**: Business users define KPIs, Ollyver implements the metrics
• **Day-2 Operations**: Automated instrumentation, logging, and monitoring setup
• **Feedback Loop**: Ops team receives troubleshooting guides and learns from real-world usage
